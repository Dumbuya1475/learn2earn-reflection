# Learn2Earn - Tell Your Story Submission

## Overview
This repository contains my submission for the Learn2Earn bounty program. It reflects my learning journey, challenges, and growth during the program.

## What I Learned
- How to complete structured tasks under deadlines
- Basics of working like a freelancer (building and submitting work)
- Importance of consistency and finishing tasks

## Biggest Challenge
My biggest challenge was consistency and managing deadlines. I often delayed tasks until close to submission time, which affected my workflow and quality.

## How I Overcame It
I started breaking tasks into smaller steps and working earlier instead of waiting for deadlines. This helped me become more consistent and improved my output.

## Next 3 Months Plan
- Build 2–3 real projects for my portfolio
- Improve my frontend/backend development skills
- Practice freelancing-style projects and workflows
- Focus on consistency and shipping work regularly
